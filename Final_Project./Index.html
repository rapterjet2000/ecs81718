BEHOLD THE NUNDU

Previous Next

Hello there, my name is Julius Linus. I'm from Chicago, Illinois. I grew up in a latino neighborhood so when I first arrived at Lane Tech I marveled at the amount of White people around me. I remember being worried about making friends when I had first arrived and being really stressed out about grades and such. If I look back their is probably a ton of cringy stuff that I did back then along with some stuff I'm not entirely proud off. If I were to look at myself back then I would be shocked at how much I had grown since then, physically and mentally. Lane Tech has been a completely new experience for me and I hope that the next generation of LTAC would cherish it just like I had.
